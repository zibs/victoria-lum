ABOUT

After completing my Bachelors of Design Honours programme in Toronto, I returned to Vancouver to work for contemporary arts organizations such as Fillip/New Documents, Project Space, and Information Office. I enjoy experimenting with other artists across multiple design disciplines, which include but are not limited to art publishing, web, identities and a little bit of furniture design.

Available for commissions, collaborations, and freelance work.

You can contact me at design@vickylum.com.
