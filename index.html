ABOUT ME

After completing my Bachelors of Design Honours programme in Toronto, I returned to Vancouver to work for local arts organizations including Fillip, New Documents and Project Space. I enjoy experimenting with other artists across multiple design disciplines, which include but are not limited to art publishing, web, identities and sometimes furniture design.

Available for commissions, collaborations, and freelance work.

You can contact me at design@vickylum.com.
